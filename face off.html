FACE OFF

For other uses, see Face Off (disambiguation). Face/Off is a 1997 American action thriller film directed by John Woo, written by Mike Werb and Michael Colleary, and starring John Travolta and Nicolas Cage. Travolta plays an FBI agent and Cage plays a terrorist, sworn enemies who assume each other's physical appearances. Music by: John Powell Produced by: David Permut, Barrie M.
