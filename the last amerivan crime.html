THE LAST AMERICAN CRIME

The Last Days of American Crime is a 2020 American action thriller film directed by Olivier Megaton from a screenplay written by Karl Gajdusek, based on Rick Remender and Greg Tocchini's 2009 graphic novel of the same name. It stars Édgar Ramírez, Anna Brewster, Michael Pitt, Patrick Bergin, and Sharlto Copley. It was panned by critics, who noted the film's release as unfortunate for coinciding with the George Floyd protests, due to its violent content and depictions of police brutality, and as of October 2021 holds a 0% rating on Rotten Tomatoes.Release dateJun 05, 2020 DirectorOlivier Megaton Story byKarl Gajdusek · Rick Remender · Greg Tocchini ScreenwritersKarl Gajdusek · Rick Remender · Greg Tocchini.
